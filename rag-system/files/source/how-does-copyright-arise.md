# How does copyright arise?

**Question:** How does copyright come into existence?


**Answer:** Copyright arises automatically once an original work is written down or recorded in some way, whether on paper, digitally, or in any other form. No registration, publication, or copyright notice is required. Material published online is protected in the same way as material published in any other format. Only original works qualify for copyright protection. In this context, originality does not mean creative brilliance. It simply means the work involved some independent skill, effort, or judgement by the author and is not a copy of someone else's work. Copyright protects the expression of ideas, not the ideas themselves.
