# Tertiary education providers' own copyright policies

**Question:** Does my institution have its own copyright policy that I need to follow?


**Answer:** Tertiary education providers are responsible for ensuring their staff and students comply with copyright rules. Most institutions have their own written copyright policies that outline what is expected of staff and students when using third party copyright material. In some cases, failure to copy and share copyright material legally may result in disciplinary action by the institution against the person responsible. You should check your institution's copyright policy in addition to understanding what is permitted under the Copyright Act and any collective or subscription licences your institution holds.
