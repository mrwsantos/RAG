# Distribution of copied material

**Question:** How can I share or distribute material I have copied under my CLNZ licence?


**Answer:** Copied material may be shared in print as handouts, class notes, lecturer notes, compiled worksheets, or other printed documents. It may be shared digitally via interactive whiteboard, on screen, by email, or through password-protected intranet sites and learning management systems. In all cases, distribution must be limited to a defined group of authorised recipients within your organisation, such as a class of students or a department. Distribution to anyone outside that defined group is not permitted.


**Related questions:** Can I email copied material to my students? Can I put copied material on our intranet or LMS? Who can I share copied material with? Can I distribute photocopied handouts to my class?
