# Preparing for data collection

**Question:** How should we prepare for a CLNZ data collection?


**Answer:** The most effective preparation for a data collection is ensuring that teaching staff know the three basic terms of the licence and understand the importance of recording accurate bibliographic information for material copied under the licence. If course materials are compiled into packs with a contents page that includes the bibliographic details of each item, the data collection process is straightforward: the information simply needs to be transferred to the CLNZ spreadsheet along with the number of students who had access to the pack. CLNZ provides resources to support staff education on this.
