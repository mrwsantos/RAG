# If the use is non-commercial, is permission still needed?

**Question:** Do I need permission to use copyright material if my use is non-commercial?


**Answer:** In most cases, yes. Non-commercial use does not automatically exempt you from needing permission. The copyright owner may be willing to grant permission more readily or on more favourable terms for non-commercial use, but permission is still generally required unless a specific exception in the Copyright Act applies to your situation.
