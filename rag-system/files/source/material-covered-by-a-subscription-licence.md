# Material covered by a subscription licence

**Question:** What are my rights when copying from a database my institution subscribes to?


**Answer:** If your institution subscribes to electronic databases, e-journals, or e-books, your rights to copy and distribute that content are governed by the specific licence agreement between your institution and the vendor, not by the Copyright Act or the CLNZ licence. Each agreement may be different and may permit more or less use than would otherwise apply. If you want to copy content from subscription databases for print course packs or provide electronic access to students, check with your institution's librarian or copyright officer before doing so.
