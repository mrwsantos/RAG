# Do copyright works need to be registered with CLNZ to receive a payment?

**Question:** Does a copyright work need to be registered with CLNZ to receive a payment?


**Answer:** No. Registration is not required to receive a payment from CLNZ. Payments are allocated based on reported usage by licenceholders. When a work is reported as being used, CLNZ researchers validate it. If your work is already recorded in the CLNZ database, the validation process is faster and easier, but being registered does not increase the likelihood of receiving a payment.
