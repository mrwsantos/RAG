# Part 6A: Enforcement officers

Enforcement officers

134A Chief executive may appoint enforcement officers

The chief executive may appoint enforcement officers, on a permanent or
temporary basis, to perform the functions set out in section 134C and exercise
the powers conferred on an enforcement officer by this Act.

Section 134A: inserted, on 7 October 2011, by section 6 of the Copyright Amendment Act 2011
(2011 No 72).

an enforcement officer.

(4) A person who ceases to be an enforcement officer must return the person’s

warrant of appointment.

(5) A person who fails to comply with subsection (4) commits an offence and is

liable on conviction to a fine not exceeding $1,000.
