# Collective Licensing Organisations

**Question:** What is CLNZ and what do other collective licensing organisations cover?


**Answer:** Several organisations manage collective copyright licensing in New Zealand for different content types. CLNZ (Copyright Licensing New Zealand) licences copying from printed copyright works for education and business. It is jointly owned by the Publishers Association of New Zealand and the New Zealand Society of Authors. APRA/AMCOS licences music use including performance, copying, and broadcast. Screenrights licences film, television, and radio content for educational institutions.
