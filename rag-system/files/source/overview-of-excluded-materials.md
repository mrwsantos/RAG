# Overview of excluded materials

**Question:** What types of material are excluded from copyright or from the CLNZ licence?


**Answer:** There are two categories of exclusions to be aware of. The first is material that is excluded from copyright protection generally: for example, ideas and information (as opposed to their expression), names, titles, and certain public documents. The second is material that is specifically excluded from the CLNZ licence even though it may be protected by copyright generally. In both cases, the absence of a copyright symbol or statement does not mean a work is unprotected. It is prudent to assume copyright applies unless there is a clear statement that it does not.
