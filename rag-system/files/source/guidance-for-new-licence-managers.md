# Guidance for new licence managers

**Question:** What do I need to know as a new CLNZ licence manager?


**Answer:** If you are new to managing a CLNZ licence, there are three key things to address. First, check that staff have received suitable guidance on how to use the licence. CLNZ provides resources to help, including licence posters that should be placed beside each photocopier or scanner used by staff and prominently on your intranet. In education settings, take care not to place these at copiers used by students, as the licence does not cover copying by students. Second, ensure there is a procedure to monitor copyright compliance and that staff know who to contact with questions. Third, carry out regular staff professional development on copyright and the licence. CLNZ can assist with resources for all of these steps.
