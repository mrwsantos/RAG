# Digital works

**Question:** Can I copy a work that is only available in digital format?


**Answer:** Licenceholders may apply to CLNZ for permission to copy a work that is only available in digital format. Applications are subject to approval and a quote will be provided via the MyCopyright Licence Portal. This service is currently available to universities and wānanga only.


**Related questions:** Does my CLNZ licence cover digital content? Can I copy an ebook or digital publication? What is the digital works permission? I need to copy something that only exists online. Is that covered?
