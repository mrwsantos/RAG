# Work in transit

**Question:** What is 'work in transit' and who can apply for it?


**Answer:** Work in transit allows licenceholders to apply to CLNZ for permission to copy from a work before it has been delivered, up to the amount of copying agreed in advance. This service is available for universities, institutes of technology/polytechnics, and wānanga. For a work in transit application to be successful, CLNZ must be able to show that the licenceholder has purchased the work but it is not yet available to copy. Evidence such as an order and delivery confirmation from the supplier would be required. Applications are made through the MyCopyright Licence Portal. Applications are subject to approval and a quote will be provided.
