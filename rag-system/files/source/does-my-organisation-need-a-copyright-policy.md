# Does my organisation need a copyright policy?

**Question:** Does my organisation need a copyright policy?


**Answer:** Yes. Copyright compliance is a legal obligation for all organisations, and every organisation should have a current copyright policy that all staff are familiar with. Your policy should address both how staff use third-party copyright material and how copyright material created within your organisation is treated. CLNZ recommends the policy be reviewed regularly and maintained by someone with specialist knowledge of copyright law.
