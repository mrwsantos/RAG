# Can a name or title be protected by copyright?

**Question:** Is a name or title protected by copyright?


**Answer:** Generally no. Copyright does not protect names or titles. Two works can share the same title without that being a copyright issue, unless one has directly copied content from the other. Names and titles may be covered by other areas of law such as trademark or passing off. If you have a concern about a specific situation involving a name or title, legal advice may be appropriate.
