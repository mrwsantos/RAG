# Data collection support and resources

**Question:** What support does CLNZ offer for data collection?


**Answer:** CLNZ offers a range of resources to help organisations complete data collection requirements. These include posters and digital resources on copyright compliance for staff and students, printed and digital guidance for library and teaching staff, template spreadsheets, e-learning modules covering both the licence and broader copyright matters, and CLNZ licence education posters. Your organisation's nominated liaison person is encouraged to contact CLNZ to discuss the best approach for your situation.
