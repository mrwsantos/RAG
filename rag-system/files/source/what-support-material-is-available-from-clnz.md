# What support material is available from CLNZ?

**Question:** What resources does CLNZ offer to help organisations manage copyright compliance?


**Answer:** CLNZ offers a range of support resources for licenceholders and their staff. These cover the CLNZ licence specifically, as well as professional development for staff and general copyright information. CLNZ's materials are designed to encourage compliance by emphasising the value of respecting copyright rather than framing it primarily as a legal obligation. Licence managers are encouraged to use the same positive tone when communicating with staff about copyright. For more information about available resources, contact CLNZ directly.
