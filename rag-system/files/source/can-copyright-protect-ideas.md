# Can copyright protect ideas?

**Question:** Does copyright protect ideas?


**Answer:** No. Copyright protects the expression of ideas: the specific way something is written, drawn, or recorded. It does not protect the ideas themselves. If someone independently creates something similar using the same idea, that is not copyright infringement. Other forms of intellectual property such as patents may apply to ideas in certain circumstances.
