# Is it hard to prove copyright infringement has occurred?

**Question:** How difficult is it to prove copyright infringement?


**Answer:** Generally, no. Copyright infringement is primarily a matter of civil law, not criminal law. Civil cases require proof on the balance of probabilities, which is a lower standard than the criminal burden of proof beyond reasonable doubt. This makes it easier for copyright owners to establish that infringement has occurred.
