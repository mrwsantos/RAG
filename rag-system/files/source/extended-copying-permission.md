# Extended copying permission

**Question:** Can I copy more than 20% or two chapters of a work?


**Answer:** Universities and wānanga may apply to CLNZ to request publishers permission to copy more than 20% or two chapters of a work. This requires the rightsholder's consent and an additional fee applies. Applications are subject to approval and a quote will be provided via the MyCopyright Licence Portal.
