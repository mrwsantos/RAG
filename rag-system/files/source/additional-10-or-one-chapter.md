# Additional 10% or one chapter

**Question:** Can I copy more than my standard CLNZ licence limit?


**Answer:** Licenceholders may apply to CLNZ for permission to copy up to 10% or one chapter beyond their standard limit, to a ceiling of 20% or two chapters. Applications are subject to approval and a quote will be provided. Apply via the MyCopyright Licence Portal. Available to universities, institutes of technology/polytechnics, wānanga, private training establishments, and primary and secondary schools.
