# Extended licencing for short courses

**Question:** Can my CLNZ licence be extended to cover short course participants?


**Answer:** Universities and wānanga may apply to extend their CLNZ licence coverage to include participants in short courses, such as executive education programmes, whose learners are not otherwise covered by the standard licence. Applications are subject to approval and a quote will be provided via the MyCopyright Licence Portal.
