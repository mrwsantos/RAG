# How to carry out data collection

**Question:** How does the CLNZ data collection process work?


**Answer:** When a data collection is due, CLNZ will give approximately three months notice and ask your Chief Executive to nominate a staff member as a copyright liaison. CLNZ will work with the liaison to prepare your organisation. The process uses a digital or hard copy spreadsheet template supplied by CLNZ, along with supporting instructions. The spreadsheet can be distributed to staff to complete individually, or the liaison person can complete it with staff input. Either way, the liaison person is responsible for ensuring the data is complete, accurate, and comprehensive. Once finished, the completed spreadsheet is submitted to CLNZ for processing.
