# If the author is credited, is permission still needed?

**Question:** Does crediting the author mean I do not need permission to use their work?


**Answer:** No. Crediting the author does not remove the need for permission. There is a separate legal obligation to credit the author in many circumstances, but this is in addition to, not instead of, getting permission. If it is not clearly stated that the work can be used freely, you should assume that both credit and permission are required.
