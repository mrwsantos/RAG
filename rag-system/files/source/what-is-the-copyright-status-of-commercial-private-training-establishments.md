# What is the copyright status of commercial private training establishments?

**Question:** Do the educational copyright exceptions apply to commercial private training establishments?


**Answer:** No. The fair dealing exception for education does not apply to commercial organisations, including commercial private training establishments (PTEs). The Copyright Act's definition of \"educational establishment\" specifically excludes organisations conducted for profit. This means that commercial PTEs cannot rely on the 3% copying allowance available under the educational exception. Commercial PTEs should check what is available under any collective or subscription licences they hold, or obtain permission from copyright owners for uses not covered by a licence.
