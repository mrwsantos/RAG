# Can't I legally copy 10% without it being infringement?

**Question:** Is there a legal right to copy 10% of a work without infringement?


**Answer:** There is no general legal right to copy 10% of a work. If your organisation holds a CLNZ licence, you may copy up to 10% provided you comply with all other licence terms. Without a CLNZ licence, there is no automatic 10% threshold -- any unauthorised copying could constitute infringement unless a fair dealing exception applies.
