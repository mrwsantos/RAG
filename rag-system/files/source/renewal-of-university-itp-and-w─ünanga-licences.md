# Renewal of university, ITP, and wānanga licences

**Question:** How are university, ITP, and wānanga licences renewed?


**Answer:** University, ITP, and wānanga licences are renewed periodically through discussions between CLNZ and representative groups. Once renewed, they run for multiple years and the terms do not change during that period unless both parties agree. In some cases, CLNZ and licenceholders may agree to trial additional licence features during the term of the licence.
