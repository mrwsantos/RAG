# How does copyright affect publishers?

**Question:** How does copyright affect publishers?


**Answer:** Publishers deal with copyright in three main ways: they use or acquire copyright material belonging to others, they create or commission new copyright material, and they grant rights in their material to others. It is important for publishers to have systems in place to manage their own copyright and their use of others' material effectively, across both traditional and digital publishing. If content is shared or copied without proper licensing, it can undermine the economic viability of the publishing industry and reduce the quality and availability of creative works.
